Section 01 — DPP Platform

Your Digital Product Passport

Select a species from the grid below, then choose your tier. Your DPP is minted as a blockchain NFT — transferable, permanent, and always connected to your animal.

The preview on the left updates live as you browse species. Guardian and Champion tiers unlock the live camera feed view.

📡

Live Feed Access

Guardian+ tiers unlock gated AI-governed camera feeds showing your species in real time.

📓

Keeper Diary

Receive regular keeper diary entries, health updates, and care records — all attached to your DPP.

🌱

Carbon Impact Tracker

Every adoption plants 1 tree and removes 1 lb plastic. Your running totals are visible on your dashboard.

🔗

Transferable Ownership

Your DPP lives in your wallet. If you transfer or sell it, all access rights move to the new holder.

Section 02 — Rent a Farm

Sustainable Aquaculture

Support sustainable food systems. Rent a modular RAS container, grow species like Sea Trout, track your yield, and receive blockchain provenance certificates for every harvest.

🐠

Modular RAS Containers

Containerised Recirculating Aquaculture Systems deployable anywhere. Real-time water quality, growth, and yield monitoring.

📜

Digital Provenance Certificate

Every harvest batch gets a blockchain-secured DPP certificate — farm-to-fork traceability for conscious consumers.

🌊

Sea Trout Release Programme

Every farm rental funds juvenile sea trout rearing and wild river release — each fish tracked by its own DPP.

🇳🇱

Netherlands & Puglia Pilots

Active deployments in Leeuwarden and two Puglia (Italy) locations. EU grant-backed consortium. Join the programme.
